    Residential District 3 (R3) is one of the largest residential neighborhoods in the New Administrative Capital and forms a major part of the city’s first phase of development. Covering roughly 1,000 feddans, the district was designed primarily to provide housing for Egypt’s middle-income residents within a planned urban environment that integrates residential buildings, services, and green public spaces. The project includes nearly 700 residential buildings and around 24,000 housing units, supporting a planned population of tens of thousands of residents. Construction began in 2018 through a collaboration between the Administrative Capital for Urban Development and China State Construction Engineering Corporation, making R3 one of the earliest large-scale residential districts completed in the new capital.
    
# R2
  ### resdential
  Area = 1,169 acres

  #### paragraph
    Residential District 2 (R2) is one of the main residential neighborhoods developed in the early stages of the New Administrative Capital. The district was planned primarily to provide organized housing for middle-income residents within a modern urban environment that integrates residential buildings with services, green areas, and community facilities. Developed under the supervision of the Administrative Capital for Urban Development as part of the capital’s first development phase, R2 contributes to establishing the city’s initial residential communities and supporting the gradual shift of population toward Egypt’s new administrative center.
